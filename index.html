What this is

A free college predictor for JEE 2026 aspirants. Built from publicly available CSAB 2025 and JoSAA 2025 closing rank data covering 23 IITs, 31 NITs, 26 IIITs, and 41 GFTIs.

Not affiliated with NTA, JoSAA, CSAB, or any institute.

How "Reach / Target / Safe" works

  • Safe — closing rank is ≥10,000 above yours
  • Target — margin is 2,000–10,000
  • Reach — margin under 2,000 (risky, may close)

Build a balanced choice list: 4–5 Safe, 3–4 Target, 2–3 Reach in preference order.

Important disclaimer

2025 cutoffs are the best signal we have for 2026, but not a guarantee. Cutoffs shift year-over-year based on the candidate pool, seat changes, and new institutes.

Verify final cutoffs on official sources before locking choices: josaa.nic.in, csab.nic.in.

What's not covered

State-level counseling (JAC Delhi for DTU/NSUT, MHT-CET for Maharashtra, KCET for Karnataka, etc.) and private institutes (BITS, VIT, Manipal, Thapar). Use those institutes' own admission processes.
